About Helios

Founded and run by students, Helios Foundation empowers campuses and communities to adopt renewable energy. We focus on practical projects that deliver measurable savings while training the next generation of clean-energy practitioners.

  • Hands-on installations
  • Capacity building for youth & SHGs
  • Energy audits & efficiency
  • Policy outreach & partnerships
Our Vision
A carbon-light campus and empowered communities.
